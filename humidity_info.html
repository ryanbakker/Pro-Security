Humidity

In winter, naturally the air is dry. This usually causes moisture to evaporate from peoples skin, making you feel more cold. With high humidity in summer, this makes you feel muggy. Which encourages the increased use in air conditioning, wasting energy. Our recommendation is to keep humidity levels between 40% to 60%.
